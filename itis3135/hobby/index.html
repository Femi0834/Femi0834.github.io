What is fishing?

Fishing is the act of catching fish by means of food or for sport

Common equipment:

  • Fishing rod
  • Bait(real or fake)
  • Scissors
  • Pilers
  • Extra hooks, weights, bait

How to Fish

Here are some simple steps

1. Cast out line of fishing rod to desired area

2. Slowly reel in after the hook sinks

3. If you feel a tug on the line, jerk the rod up to hook fish

4. Continue to reel til you get the fish out of the water
