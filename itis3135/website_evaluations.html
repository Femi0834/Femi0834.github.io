Website Evaluations

Amazon

Amazon is an online retailer that provides a variety of products like appeal, beauty and health, jewellery, electronics, grocery, books, and games.

  • C(ontrast): All elements of the page stand out from each other. No color clashes with another making it very easy to read.
  • R(epetition): Overall layout is consitent throughout the page.
  • A(lignment): Cohesive in the alignment of all elements.
  • P(roximity): Organized to enhance the comprehension of elements, similar elements are in close proximity to each other.

Craigslist

Craigslist is a website for viewing and posting local advertisements, users can find jobs, apartments, used cars, and personal ads as well as a lot more.

  • C(ontrast): The tone of the website is dull.
  • R(epetition): The style does remain consistent through out the page. All elements are hyperlinks
  • A(lignment): Title is small and aligned to the left side of the page.
  • P(roximity): All elements are condensed under its category in a linear line down over two columns
